Betusline Kids Rain Jacket

Keep your little one dry and comfortable in style with the Betusline Kids Rain Jacket. This lightweight raincoat is designed for kids aged 2 to 14, making it the perfect companion for everyday adventures, school days, or unexpected rain showers.

Featuring a waterproof and windproof liner, this jacket ensures protection from the elements without compromising on breathability. The soft plaid lining provides extra warmth and a cozy feel, while the durable materials are built to last through daily wear and tear.

With two handy front pockets and a removable hood, it's easy to store essentials and stay ready for any weather condition. The unique ruched waist design adds a flattering fit and comfort, helping your child move freely all day long.
